Why LabMate?

LabMate is not just another e-learning platform. We are purpose-built for laboratory education, combining pedagogy, technology, and institutional support.

Safe & Risk-Free Experiments

Conduct dangerous chemical or electrical experiments without any physical risk to students or infrastructure.

24 / 7 Anytime Access

Students can repeat experiments at any hour, from any device, accelerating learning at their own pace.

Cost-Effective for Institutions

Eliminate recurring costs of lab equipment, chemicals, and maintenance with a single annual subscription.

Curriculum-Aligned Content

Every experiment maps directly to university syllabi, ensuring academic compliance and exam readiness.

Performance Analytics

Instructors get real-time dashboards tracking student progress, completion rates, and assessment scores.

Multi-Branch Engineering Labs

Supports CSE, ECE, Mechanical, Civil, and more — a single platform for the entire campus.
